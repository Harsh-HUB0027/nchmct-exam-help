Exam Syllabus & Pattern

The NCHMCT exam covers subjects like:

  • English Language & Comprehension
  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs
  • Reasoning & Analytical Ability
  • Numerical Ability & Aptitude
  • Scientific Aptitude

Exam Pattern: 200 multiple-choice questions, 3 hours duration.
