Global Internship Roadmap

🎓

Year 1: Learn basics of Food Production, Front Office, F&B Service, Housekeeping.

🏨

Year 2: Industrial Training in India (Taj, Oberoi, ITC, Marriott, etc.).

✈️

Year 3: Apply for International Internship in Australia, Dubai, Switzerland, USA.

💼

Year 4: Final Placement – Many students get absorbed abroad after internship.

Career Path After Hotel Management Abroad

Australia 🇦🇺

Jobs: Chef, Barista, Front Office Manager

Salary: AUD $45k – $60k/year

Extra: Pathway to PR after 2–3 years

Dubai 🇦🇪

Jobs: Hotel Ops, Guest Relations

Salary: AED 3,000 – 7,000/month

Extra: Accommodation & food perks

Switzerland 🇨🇭

Jobs: Fine Dining Chef, Resort Manager

Salary: CHF 3,500 – 6,000/month

Extra: Global career growth

USA 🇺🇸

Jobs: Restaurant Manager, Cruise Jobs

Salary: USD $30k – $50k/year

Extra: J1 Visa cultural exchange
