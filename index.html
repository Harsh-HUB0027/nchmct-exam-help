Detailed Exam Syllabus

The NCHMCT JEE consists of 200 questions divided into 5 sections:

  • English Language: Reading comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, para jumbles, cloze test, error spotting, idioms & phrases.
  • Reasoning & Logical Deduction: Series, coding–decoding, blood relations, directions, syllogisms, analogies, puzzles.
  • Numerical Ability & Analytical Aptitude: Percentages, profit & loss, averages, ratios, time–speed–distance, simple/compound interest, mensuration, data interpretation.
  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs: National/international news, history, geography, economy, sports, awards, hospitality industry updates.
  • Aptitude for Service Sector: Situational judgement in hospitality, ethics, team-work, customer orientation, decision-making.

Marking scheme: +4 for correct, -1 for incorrect. Duration: 3 hours (CBT).
